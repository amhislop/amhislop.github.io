Free eBooks of the Bible's
teaching & topical Bible studies.

The Bible Unpacked (TBU) is the name of seven free eBooks that present the Bible’s teaching — with Bible verses. They cater for non-Christians through to mature Christians.

Each of the seven eBooks is an abbreviated edition of the next longest one. They are available in ePub, mobi and PDF formats – and also in online versions, which can be downloaded.

Intermediate Edition

The Bible’s teaching summarised for growing believers

Comprehensive Edition

The Bible’s teaching compiled for established believers

In-Depth Edition

The Bible’s teaching expounded for Bible students
